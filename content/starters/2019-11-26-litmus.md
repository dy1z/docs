## Templates

The following templates are included:

### Slate

- Newsletter
- Product Update
- Receipt
- Simple Announcement
- Stationery

### Ceej

- Account Update
- Expired Card
- Password Reset
- New Account
- Closed Account

Each Ceej template comes in 3 versions:

- HTML
- MailChimp
- Campaign Monitor
